Prabhav 2024

Impact Beyond Returns: The India Story

The Impact Investors Council (IIC) successfully concluded Prabhav 2024, India's premier impact investing conclave, on the 12th & 13th of September, 2024. Held under the theme of "Impact Beyond Returns: The India Story", this edition focused on both the social and market dimensions of impact investing.

Social Impact Imperative

Equipped attendees with insights to address pressing social issues such as climate change, education, healthcare, gender inclusivity and the achievement of the sustainable development goals.

Market Imperative

The conclave explored innovative financing strategies and navigated the evolving policy landscape to diversify the capital base, institutionalize market-based solutions, and mainstream impact investing within the private sector.

Audience Attended

Prabhav 2024 attracted a diverse range of stakeholders, including industry leaders from organizations like SIDBI, Canopy, BII, MSDF and many others. This esteemed gathering fostered a dynamic exchange of ideas and a commitment to advancing India's impact investing ecosystem.

Biennial Event

India Green Investment Forum 2023

India's First-of-its-kind gathering for Green Finance stakeholders

India's First-of-its-kind gathering for Green Finance stakeholders to elevate the conversation from well-understood sectors to the next wave of innovation, scale, and impact. The event was hosted at The Grand, New Delhi on the 6th and 7th of November 2023. With over 530 attendees and 100 speakers from 18 countries, the event catalyzed discussions, unlocked new possibilities and drove impactful change towards a greener, more sustainable world.

Key Themes

Deep dive into unexplored sectors

  • Green Hydrogen

  • Battery Energy Storage Systems

  • Waste Management & Material Circularity

  • Climate Smart Agriculture

Plenaries during the session focused on

  • Climate Adaptation Finance

  • Blended Finance Construct For Mobilising Green Capital

  • Impact Assessment Framework For Climate Investments

  • Pathways For 'Greening' India's Corporate

  • Exploring The Policy Paradigm for 'Green Finance'

Targeted Outcomes

Build a coherent narrative for investment strategy in the sector for different forms of capital - Equity, Debt, Grant and Blended Finance

Drive increased participation of Catalytic Capital for scaling up innovations in climate impact

Demonstrate avenues for collaborations and partnerships across the ecosystem. Foster conversations between industry leaders

The India Room at IGIF

The Breakfast Session with LPs and The Family Office Session

As a series of side events to IGIF, IIC organized a Breakfast Session with LPs and a dedicated Family Office Session. The India Room played host to over 20 LPs, fostering meaningful discussions and connections across both sessions. With multiple India Room chapters across various geographies, this homecoming at IGIF marked another milestone in its impactful journey.

Beyond the Forum

IGIF extended its impact through insightful publications and research:

Green Investment Opportunities in India

This comprehensive report, co-authored by IIC and Climate Policy Initiative (CPI), supported by Neev Funds, identifies high-growth sectors for green investment, backed by data and expert analysis.

Dive into the research findings and recommendations of sectors such as:

  • Green Hydrogen

  • Battery Energy Storage Systems

  • Waste Management & Material Circularity

  • Climate Smart Agriculture

Climate Tech Investment Trends in India: 2022 In Retrospect

This annual report by IIC, supported by Neev Funds, provides a deep dive into 2022's climate tech investment landscape.

Gain valuable insights on:

  • Investment trends and sector analysis

  • Innovative solutions and emerging business models

  • Recommendations for future growth

Reflecting on Past Editions: Prabhav in Retrospect

Explore the evolution of Prabhav, our flagship event series that has shaped impact investing in India over the years.

2020

Telling India's Story as the Impact Lab of the World

• Key Themes: Impact Investing During Current Times, Sectoral Insights (Agriculture, Healthcare, Financial Inclusion), Innovations in Impact Finance

• Participation: 700+ participants from 25+ countries, 140+ speakers

• Networking: Over 150 structured LP-GP meetings facilitated

2018

How Indian Impact Investing Can Help Achieve the SDGs

• Key Partnerships: IIC, Omidyar Network, MEA, NABARD, Niti Aayog

• Participants: 300+ LPs and GPs from around the world

• Focus: Strengthening collaboration between Indian and International Impact Investing Community

• Highlight Event: Hosted the Global Steering Group (GSG) Annual Summit

2016

Fostering LP-GP Relationships

• Organized by IIC in collaboration with MEA's ITP Division

• Global Participation: 180+ LPs (Limited Partners) and GPs (General Partners) from around the world

• Format: Three-day event including field trips, conference sessions, and multilateral discussions

• Focus: Building stronger LP-GP partnerships

• Special Feature: Matchmaking platform connecting Indian GPs with global and local LPs
