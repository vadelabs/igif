About IGIF 2025 registration, tickets, and event details.

IGIF 2025 will take place on October 30-31, 2025, at The Grand in New Delhi, India. The event will run from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on both days.

  • This ticket is non-refundable & non-transferrable.
  • All registration information collected will remain private and confidential.
  • Registration information will be used for the limited purpose of event communication and other IIC outreach, as opted by you in the registration form.
  • If the organizers decide to reschedule the event due to an unforeseen event, the delegate tickets will be valid for the new dates for their own use only.

  • Inclusions: Access to IGIF Event, Refreshments & Snacks, Lunch and access to Conference Application.
  • Exclusions: Accommodation, Travel Costs.
  • Invitation to join the conference application will be sent before 30-45 days prior to IGIF 2025. In case of any queries, please contact [email protected].
